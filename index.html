About

ace.radio is more than a station. It's a vision brought to life.

Founded by Luke Henwood and Christopher Marshall, the idea was born in July 2025 and officially launched just 60 days later, from start of build, on the 3rd of October 2025. With a driven and highly motivated team, ace.radio was built from the ground up to reimagine what radio could be in the digital age.

Unlike traditional platforms, ace.radio gives South Africans free streaming without using your personal data. No limits. No barriers. Just music, entertainment and connection – wherever you are.

From our state-of-the-art studios in Century City, Cape Town, we deliver a curated mix of artists, songs, and original shows every single day. Our professional presenters bring energy, humour and insight, keeping our audience informed and entertained.

For advertisers, ace.radio is more than airtime – it's precision. With real-time analytics, targeted campaigns, and transparent reporting, we put your brand directly in front of the right consumers, at the right time.
